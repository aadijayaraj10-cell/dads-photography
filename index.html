About

The Photographer

A lifelong observer of light, shadow, and the quiet drama of the natural world.

Based in the heart of the Midwest, he has spent decades wandering landscapes at dusk and dawn — chasing the fleeting moments where light transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary. His work is a meditation on patience, presence, and the beauty hiding in plain sight.

Available for fine-art prints and commissioned landscape work.
